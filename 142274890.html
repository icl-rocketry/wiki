INFO FOR GENERAL MEMBERS:

How to send in purchase requests

IMPORTANT

Only send in a purchase request if you are sure this is what you want to buy (check with your subteam lead before placing the request)!

If you send in a request but no longer need the component requested, inform the treasurer (Schnaider Fridman Ferreira Pinto, Thiago) through slack ASAP so the order can be put on hold/cancelled.


Two ways of making component purchase requests:

  1. For small number of items, make individual requests through the Google Forms https://forms.gle/LtMZAdtEdF72ap8U8
  2. For larger number of items, ask your subteam lead to fill in the Google Sheets directly (only leads have access to it to avoid confusion )

INTERNAL ORDER CODES

The treasurer will assign a unique internal code to each purchase request, with the goal of facilitating tracking, discussion, and relaying of info of ongoing orders. The system is pretty self-explanatory, with the code depending on the subteam it refers to, but a table is shown below.

Some important rules

  • Only a limited number of people from the team are allowed to purchase items and claim them. For the 2018 - 2019 and 2019 - 2020 years, only Harayama, Sena and Harradence, William can purchase items.
  • DO NOT purchase items of more than 100 pounds if using your own money (method 1). This is a strict college rule that must be restricted.
  • Get all transactions and expense claims approved by Academic supervisor Dr. Aaron Knoll.
  • For expense claims, try to do them by small chunks instead of lumping them into a large amount of money to claim at once, as that may attract the attention from the Finance people.

Step-by-step guide

Purchase with your own money, then claim

  1. Will and Sena who have rights to claim for the 2019-2020 academic year.
  2. Purchase using your own credit card.
  3. Upload a copy of the invoice to the "Claims" folder under 
  4. Fill in claim form (by hand) and bring hard printed copies of the invoices to her directly at her office.
  5. Keep the invoices/receipts make sure they contain VAT number (not payment confirmation, preferably a proper Invoice eg. amazon request an invoice on the website)
  6. One month after purchase date to claim 


Request a VCC

  1. Send request to Ela, and CC dr Knoll.
  2. Link to BOM + details on the email + screenshot or proof of price.
  3. Send invoice/proof of payment once transaction is proceeded.
