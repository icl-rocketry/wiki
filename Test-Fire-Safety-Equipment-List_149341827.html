ICL Rocketry Wiki : Test-Fire Safety Equipment List

Created by Geragidis, Filippos, last modified on Jan 29, 2020

This article provides a checklist for the necessary Safety equipment for all test-fires conducted at Silwood Park Campus.

All items on the following list must be transported to Imperial College Silwood Park Campus for all test-fire events:

  • Polycarbonate visor (1)
  • Ear defenders (appropriate amount, according to number of attendees - minimum (5 pairs))
  • Insulating gloves (5)
  • First Aid Kit (1)
  • High-vis vests (appropriate amount, according to number of attendees - minimum (5))
