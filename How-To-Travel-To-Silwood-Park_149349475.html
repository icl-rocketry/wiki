By Train and Bus/Walk


Part 1: Train from London to Sunningdale

There are three train station options for leaving london : listed below with suggeted methods to travel to each one.

  • Clapham Junction - Overground, Or Buses 345 or 49 from South Ken.
  • Putney Stn - District line: East Putney, Or Buses 414, 14, or 430 from South Ken.
  • Richmond - District Line.

IMPORTANT!! Buy a ticket before getting on the train.

  • Take a South Western train towards Reading
  • Get off at Sunningdale Train Station
  • Return trains towards London Waterloo

Trains run twice per hour, see South Western Railway for details: https://www.southwesternrailway.com/

An Anytime Day Return Ticket from Clapham Junct to Sunningdale is £8.60 with a railcard (Jan 2020).

Part 2.a. Bus From Sunningdale to Silwood Park

Depending on the time of day  you can chose to travel from Sunningdale to Silwood Park campus via bus or by walking.

Take White Bus route 01 from Sunningdale Broomhall Lane bus stop, towards Windsor. Return buses: same route towards Ascot


Route from Sunningdale Station to Bromhall Lane Bus stop:


Buses leave from Sunningdale (Broomhall Lane) at 09:13, 10:43, 12:13, 12:58, & 13:58 on weekdays and 9:13, 11:28, 13:13, & 14:58 on Saturdays and take 6 minutes.

Return Buses leave Silwood park at 12:17, 14:02, 16:32, 17:32, & 18:22 on weekdays and 12:32, 14:17, 16:32, & 18:22 on Saturdays

An Adult Day Return ticket is £3.10

All prices and times corect Jan 2020 (source: https://www.whitebus.co.uk/bus-services/01/)
