ICL Rocketry Wiki : Bulkhead/Payload mount updated Wonwoo+Vincenzo

Created by Schiavo, Vincenzo, last modified by Choi, Wonwoo on Feb 01, 2020

Short description:

  1. Joins payload to rocket, Payload needs no connection to other components
  2. Needs to hold payload in place, Non-load bearing
  3. Easy & Quick to disassemble on-fieldÂ 
  4. Est. Mass for Current Iteration: 690g
  5. Easily manufacturable (Laser cut bulkead, COTS L bars)

Assembly Procedure:

  1. Screw bottom bulkhead to bottom of payload (4 screws)
  2. Screw top bulkhead to top of payload
  3. Slide Payload+Mount assembly down into the rocket
  4. Mount Nosecone on top and screw it in place

-Two laser cut wood bulkheads, 6in diameter

-Height: 350mm
-25mm*25mm*50mm aluminium L bars, 3mm thick
-bulkheads 25 mm thick each (50 counting the piece of L bar)

-L bar holes: 10mm, Bulkhead holes 8mm
