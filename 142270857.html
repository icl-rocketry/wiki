HMSS Pierce Brosnan(17 November 2019)(1018)
(486)

Third rocket designed, first ever for a lot of new members (2019 recruits) of ICLR. Objective was to test two critical components intended to fly on ICLR’s HMSS Sporadic Impulse: the avionics eggtimer and a pyrodex-triggered CO2 ejection system. Also designed to be able to fly with a pyrodex charge, as well as a mechanical CO2 system. Custom-made CO2 system was supposed to be built and ground tested, however the development did not get completed on time for the launch.
